IProcedures
Summary
Access to the procedures and procedure functions of Visum.
Object Model
Description

 All properties are arranged below the objects IOperations and IFunctions respectively. These correspond to the settings made on the pages [Procedure Sequence] and [General Procedure settings] in the Procedures view. For reasons of clarity the whole hierarchy is shown under these leading objects.
